Frequently Asked Questions

What is GreenBite's mission? +

GreenBite is dedicated to promoting healthy living through balanced nutrition, regular exercise, and mindfulness practices. We provide science-based tools and resources to help individuals achieve their wellness goals in a sustainable and enjoyable way.

Are the recipes suitable for special dietary needs? +

Yes! Our recipe collection includes options for various dietary preferences including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and low-carb diets. Each recipe includes detailed nutritional information and dietary tags to help you find meals that fit your specific needs.

How accurate is the calorie calculator? +

Our calculator uses the scientifically validated Mifflin-St Jeor equation for BMR calculation, which is considered one of the most accurate methods available. However, individual metabolism can vary, so results should be used as guidelines alongside professional medical advice.
